Pesto Pasta

How to Make Pesto Pasta?

This simple recipe for pasta with cottage cheese came from the Polish side of my family. We call it "cottage cheese and noodles" or "lazy man pierogies." It's a great comfort food and can be made with any kind of noodle. You can serve it as a side dish, but we always enjoy it as a main course.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Start with your favorite pasta shape.
  • 1 Onion and oil: Cook the onion in olive oil until it's translucent.
  • 1 Pesto: Use store-bought or homemade pesto sauce.
  • 1 Seasonings: This pesto pasta is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • Cheese: Grate your own Parmesan cheese instead of using the pre-shredded stuff.
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  3. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat. .
